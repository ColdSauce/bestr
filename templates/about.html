This is my (Stefan Aleksic) [very alpha version] submission to the House of Representatives app competition! Basically, the app searches through 200 most recent tweets containing the text input given by the user and uses a sophisticated machine learning algorithm to average the "happiness" of each tweet. The most happy tweets are 10/10, the least happy are 0/10.

Although 200 is a quite smalll representative sample, anything more than 200 tweets would take far too much time.

This was created using: Flask, Python, HTML, CSS, and a little bit of dark magic.
